Accommodation

Listed below is our accommodation for the 4-5 days. For the first and last night, the group will be split across the Manor and Caer Rhur Hall hotels, but will join together at the Manor for the evening meal

The Manor

Address: Gorse Hill Caravan Park, Trefriw Road, Conwy, LL32 8HJ

A beautifully restored 19th-century manor house. It blends historic charm with oak-panelled fireplaces and real fires with a contemporary feel and luxurious en-suite rooms.

Visit Website

Caer Rhur Hall Hotel

Address: Caer Rhun Hall Hotel, Conwy LL32 8HX

A stunning Victorian manor nestled in the lush countryside of Conwy. Surrounded by 18 acres of gardens and the dramatic beauty of Snowdonia, it offers a tranquil escape with elegant rooms, modern amenities, and timeless charm.

The Lion Hotel

Address: Y Maes, Criccieth, Gwynedd LL52 0AA

Located near Criccieth Castle, this welcoming hotel offers scenic views and comfortable rooms.
