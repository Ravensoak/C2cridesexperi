Install C2C Rides as an App

Follow the instructions below to install this site as an App on your Android device.

  1. Open c2crides.netlify.app in Google Chrome on your Android device.
  2. Tap the three dot menu (⋮) in the top right corner of Chrome
  3. Choose "Install app" or "Add to Home screen".
  4. Tap "Add" → choose where you want the icon.
  5. Done ✅ You now have an icon on your home page for a one-tap opening of the C2C Rides site.
